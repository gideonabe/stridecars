The Restomod Philosophy

Engineering a legend for the modern era.

The "Midnight" project was conceived with a singular goal: to preserve the aggressive silhouette of the 1969 Dodge Charger while infusing it with the technical prowess of a modern hypercar.

Underneath the bespoke matte carbon-fiber bodywork lies a Hellephant Crate engine producing a staggering 1,000 HP. Every component, from the Brembo carbon-ceramic brakes to the custom multilink suspension, has been tuned for refined performance—a true embodiment of "Quiet Luxury" in a muscle car shell.
